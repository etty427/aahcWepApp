About AAHC

AAHC assists you with anything from walks in the park to staying in your home. We provide professional compassionate care for yourself or your loved one. We offer full range of hourly as well as live-in services. 24hr services to help start and stay on the best manageable path for your optimal health.

AAHC provides home-based private care providers to assist with necessary, daily activities. We understand that your home is filled with memories and your home is a place where children, grandchildren and friends come to enjoy precious moments. There is no other place you'd rather be, and AAHC want to make sure you spend your time safe and comfortable.

We are currently taking new clients/patients to provide families and individuals with the best care. We start by discussing your needs and wishes with you and family members. AAHC works with you and your family to put a plan together to take care of your needs. To speak with a staff member or make an appointment please call 860-348-8936 Call us today!
